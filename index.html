We Are Family

Mission Statement:

80% of households in america are single parent households. On average it costs to 18,865 U.S dollars to have a child in america. We believe all children deserve a chance at success. Children are all born innocent, It's our duty to make sure each and every child thrives. Our purpose is to provide support, encouragement and guidance to single parents in the pursuit of identifying life choice options that enhance their opportunities for career advancement and independence.
